Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Aura St. Claire ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website aurastclaire.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our site.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. If you choose to connect your Pinterest account, we store an OAuth access token provided by Pinterest on your behalf. We do not store your Pinterest password.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email and password solely to authenticate you on this site. Your Pinterest access token is used exclusively to publish Pins to your Pinterest boards at your explicit request. We do not access, read, or modify any Pinterest data beyond what is necessary to publish the Pin you selected.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the service (e.g. Cloudflare for hosting). Pinterest integration is governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at social@aurastclaire.com.

5. Security

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are only used to perform actions you explicitly initiate.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at social@aurastclaire.com.
